To pursue a vocational certificate at HVTI, applicants need secondary education completion, proof of identity, and English proficiency. Submission of academic transcripts, a valid ID, and a passport-sized photo is required. An interview or entrance exam may be part of the admission process.

Prospective students aiming for a vocational certificate at HVTI must achieve a minimum grade of C4-D1 in the required subjects. Academic transcripts reflecting proficiency in these subjects are crucial for admission, ensuring candidates possess the foundational knowledge necessary for their chosen vocational course.
